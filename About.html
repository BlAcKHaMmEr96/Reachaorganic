Rooted in Nature

REACHA Organics began with a single leaf — matcha, harvested from the serene hills of Japan and brought to Lebanon with care. Our mission is to reconnect people with nature’s purity.

Harvested with Purpose

We source our matcha from small, sustainable farms in Japan. Each leaf is hand-picked and stone-ground to preserve its vibrant color, rich flavor, and potent antioxidants.

From Japan to Lebanon

Our matcha is imported directly to Lebanon, ensuring freshness and quality. Every batch is sealed with authenticity and delivered with a promise of wellness.

Crafted for Conscious Living

REACHA is more than a product — it’s a lifestyle. Our matcha supports mindful energy, clean nutrition, and a deeper connection to the earth. Sip with intention.
